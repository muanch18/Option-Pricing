\section{Terms}
DTE represents the Days Till Expiry. The ranges for the ratio of S to K are defined as: 
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Deep Out the Money: $0.2 < \frac{S}{K} < 0.8$
    \item At the Money: $0.8 < \frac{S}{K} < 1.2$
    \item Deep In the Money: $1.2 < \frac{S}{K} < 1.8$
\end{enumerate}
